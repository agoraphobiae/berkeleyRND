Events

Our aim is to build an environment where undergraduates can discuss and collaborate on research and development projects. To forge a community among students of varying fields and interests, we hold several social events throughout the year for members to meet and have fun!

  • Welcoming

    At the beginning of each semester, we invite all members to get together and pitch ideas for new projects while enjoying great food.

  • Project Showcase

    At the end of the year, we invite all members to get together and present what they have accomplished.
